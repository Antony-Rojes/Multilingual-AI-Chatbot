About this project

Vocalize is a Multilingual AI Voice Translator built to bridge language barriers in real time. Speak in your native tongue — the agent captures your voice, processes it through Groq's blazing-fast LLM inference, and speaks a natural-sounding reply back in the same language in a fraction of a second. Powered by the Llama 3.3 70B model and ElevenLabs' multilingual v2 voice engine, Vocalize supports 13+ languages including Tamil, Hindi, Arabic, Japanese, and more — enabling genuine cross-language dialogue without switching apps or manual translation.
